Old-world charm,
Modern luxury

Out of a love and passion for all things old, Brakdakkie pioneered the unique décor style of old-world Karoo charm combined with modern luxuries in the most incredible Karoo setting. Brakdakkie is an experience that will be the highlight of your stay in the Karoo.

Award-winning

Brakdakkie has been the recipient of: Outstanding Award from Rooms for Africa (2018), Best Value Establishment from afristay (2017), Top Accomodation from lekkeslaap (2016), and Certificate of Excellence from Trip Advisor (2014 & 2015).

Child and pet friendly

Children are most welcome at Brakdakkie, and will enjoy the open spaces and feeding the free range chickens and ducks. Extra mattresses for children are available at no extra charge. We are also pet-friendly! Please let us know ahead of time if your pet(s) will be joining you, and see below for selected policies.

Historic

Brakdakkie was originally built in 1850. Once the barns and donkey shed of the original farm, Sonskyn, the rooms have now been filled with treasures from years gone by and restored with great attention to detail, preserving the original splendour. Brakdakkie is located within walking distance of all shops and restaurants.

Amenities

Each of our four cottages offers:

  • luxurious en-suite bathrooms with towels, shower gel and shampoo
  • bedrooms with crisp, white linen
  • fully-equipped kitchenettes
  • private patios with barbecue/braai area
  • private splash pools
  • secure off-street parking

Your pets are very welcome at Brakdakkie! But please take note of the following:

  1. If your dog is able/inclined to jump fences, please inform us before booking.
  2. Keep your pets on a leash at all times when walking around the property, as we have our own dogs, cats, chickens, ducks, children, and other guests to consider. They can be let free when in your enclosed garden.
  3. Pets are charged at a rate of R50 per day. The fee goes towards extra cleaning of hair, cleaning of their ablutions on the property, spraying of carpets for fleas and ticks, and possible damage to property.
  4. Pets are not allowed to be locked in the room if you intend to be away for extended periods. Consider taking them with you. Alternatively, you either need to let them out at regular intervals, or the cottage door needs to be left open so to allow them access to the garden.
  5. Pets are not allowed on the furniture, so please bring their own bedding as well as water and food bowls.
  6. Please clean up after your pets.
  7. Pets are accommodated at their owner’s risk.
